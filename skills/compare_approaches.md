# compare_approaches

USE WHEN: You have findings from multiple papers/sources on a related sub-topic and need
a side-by-side comparison table.

## TEMPLATE

You are comparing research approaches on the topic: {topic}.

FINDINGS FROM MULTIPLE SOURCES:
{findings_text}

Produce a markdown comparison table with these columns:
| Source | Method | Strengths | Weaknesses | Domain |

Below the table, write a 3-sentence synthesis of what the comparison reveals. Cite
sources by their [source_id] markers. Be factual; if you can't determine a cell, write
"—".
